# GolfIQ Privacy Policy

Effective date: 21 June 2026

GolfIQ is a golf score tracking app designed to help users record golf rounds, view score history, and track simple performance statistics.

## Information We Collect

GolfIQ does not require users to create an account and does not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or location data.

The app allows users to enter golf-related information, such as:

-Course name

-Round date

-Score

-Player name or display name, if entered by the user



This information is stored locally on the user’s device.

## How Information Is Used

The information entered into GolfIQ is used only to display round history, statistics, and progress inside the app.

GolfIQ does not sell, share, or transfer user data to third parties.

## Local Data Storage

GolfIQ stores app data locally on the user’s device. This data is not uploaded to an external server by GolfIQ.

Users can delete their saved rounds from within the app. Uninstalling the app may also remove locally stored app data from the device.

## Internet Access

GolfIQ may request basic internet access as part of standard app functionality or platform requirements, but the app does not use this access to upload personal golf data to a server.

## Children’s Privacy

GolfIQ is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. If a parent or guardian believes that a child has provided personal information, they can remove the app or clear the app data from the device.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
